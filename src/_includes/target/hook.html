Truly good food

4P defines “good food” as something more than just local. To us, good food is: transparently sourced so you know where it came from, healthy to nourish both body and mind, ethically raised with no souls exploited in its production, and harvested in a way that maintains the health of the environment. Most of all, good food is equitably accessible to all people, regardless of who they are or where they live.

At 4P, we believe in a world where good food is the rule—not the exception. And every day, we get a little closer.

Who else are you helping by choosing 4p Foods?

The 4P mission is about more than just food. It’s about making a lasting impact on our community, our economy, and our environment. We make it easy for people and companies to give back.

Local farmers

4P currently empowers more than 200 local family farms to put their high quality produce, meat, and dairy products on more tables every day.

Low‑access communities

4P donates every tenth bag to a local shelter—giving less advantaged families more access to fresh, healthy food.

The environment

Locally sourced food requires less travel, reducing greenhouse gas emissions and improving your carbon footprint.

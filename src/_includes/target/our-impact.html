Who else Do you help when you choose 4p?

At 4P, we believe access to good food is a right, not a privilege. Our mission is about more than just food—it’s about making a lasting impact on our community, our economy, and our environment.

Local farmers

4P currently empowers more than 200 local family farms to put their high quality produce, meat, and dairy products on more tables every day.

Low‑access communities

4P fights to build a better food system that enables all people, regardless of income or ZIP code, to have access to good, healthy food. 

The environment

4P Foods works with farmers who are committed to low-impact, sustainable methods of production. Plus, local food requires less travel, improving your carbon footprint.

--- title: Food is Medicine ---

Food is Medicine:

1 in 4 dollars spent in the entire health care system is spent on treating diabetes – a disease that is 100% treatable with Good Food.

Fresh Farmacy

In 2018, 73% of participants reported they would definitely buy more fruits and vegetables after the program, while 82% would definitely cook more often at home.

Six Charlottesville-area health clinics prescribe patients a biweekly share of produce from 4P Foods, with 290 patients served so far.

Along with food, participants receive education and engagement around cooking and eating well at home.

New-to-wholesale farmers provide the fruits and vegetables for each delivery, giving them a valuable introduction to growing at wholesale scale.

Impact assessed through biometric data from clinics; pre-, mid-, and post-season surveys; and end of season focus groups.

Our Blue Ocean Opportunity

The healthcare and insurance industries increasingly recognize that Food is Medicine, and a powerful one at that. Expanding on our successful Fresh Farmacy program, we are poised to capture this largely untapped market of food as a preventative health tool that is not only sought out by the few, but prescribed to the many via the healthcare system.

Opportunity: Fruit and Veggie Rx programs for patients at risk of or suffering from diet-related diseases via B2C channel.

Opportunity: wholesale distribution to hospital cafeterias and meals programs via B2B channel.

Opportunity: Produce Plus/Market Bucks (farmers’ market incentive) programs via SNAP redemption

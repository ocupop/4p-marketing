Impact to farmers

To date, we have linked 200+ farmers with 4,000+ consumers and nearly 150 wholesale buyers {' '} (since LFH merge in June 2019).

We are paying farmers fair prices for Good Food while maintaining strong unit economics.

In the 4P Foods supply chain, farmers make on average 60% of every B2C sale and 80% of every B2B sale .

In the industrial food supply chain, farmers make 8% on every food dollar (USDA ERS data).

We add around $20 to the bottom line with every B2C delivery and $100 with every B2B delivery (projected to increase to $25 and $225 by 2023 through economies of scale and efficiency improvements).

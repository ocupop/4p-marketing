Business Model

4P Foods buys food from a network of food hubs up and down the Eastern Seaboard and sells that food through B2C and B2B channels.

Consumers throughout the DMV purchase via a fully customizable recurring subscription. →  Over 750 weekly subscribers, with strong contribution margins .

Addition of B2B channel opens up new client base of wholesale and institutional buyers. →  Creates strong unit economics on last-mile distribution .
